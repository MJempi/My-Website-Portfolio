Data Science Skills

Technical Skills

  • Programming: Proficiency in Python and R for data analysis, machine learning, and automation.
  • SQL: Strong knowledge of querying databases and handling structured data.
  • Statistics & Probability: Understanding concepts like hypothesis testing, probability distributions, and statistical modeling.
  • Machine Learning: Knowledge of supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation, and algorithms like regression, decision trees, and neural networks.
  • Data Wrangling: Cleaning, transforming, and structuring raw data for analysis.
  • Data Visualization: Proficiency with Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI, or Looker for storytelling with data.

Analytical & Problem-Solving Skills

  • Critical Thinking: The ability to approach problems logically and find the best solutions.
  • Domain Knowledge: Understanding the industry (e.g., finance, healthcare, e-commerce) to apply data insights effectively.
  • A/B Testing & Experimentation: Designing and analyzing experiments for data-driven decision-making.
  • Mathematics: Linear algebra, calculus, and optimization for building accurate models.

Soft Skills

  • Communication: Explaining complex data insights to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Storytelling with Data: Presenting insights in a compelling and actionable way.
  • Collaboration: Working with engineers, business analysts, and decision-makers.
